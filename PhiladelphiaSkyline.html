What's Happening in Your Skyline?

By: Joey McDougall | March 8,2016

Anyone who has ever lived in a big city has undoubtedly looked out at the massive skyscrapers that pierce through the horizon. These buildings are a testament to mankind's desire to go bigger and better, and humanities refusal to settle for what's within reach. However, it doesn't often cross one's mind what actually goes on within these buildings. While it may not be as popular as New York City or Las Vegas, the Philadelphia remains one of America's most beloved skylines. The following photo will allow you to explore what goes one within the buildings that create Philadelphia's skyline, as well as some other fun facts along the way.
